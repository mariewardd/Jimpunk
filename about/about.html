“Jimpunk is great in that you say let’s do this, and he’s like a machine. He just starts doing it.” 
- Antonio Mendoza
Jimpunk is an anonymous French Artist going by the pseudonym Jimpunk. A well respected net artist who takes surrealist techniques and punk styles in their websites and art. Jimpunk uses datamoshing, data culture, collage, and a lot of stimulating images, sounds, and videos to entice the viewer into the various websites that they've created. The anonymity of Jimpunk only adds to the concept of their online art. Many of their pieces such as Screenfull and Dysleksic (yes, that is how it is spelled) are similar to what a computer virus might look like with the loud, clashing noises and ovverlapping collage style.
Marie Ward
